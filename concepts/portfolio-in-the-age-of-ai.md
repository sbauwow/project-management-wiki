# Portfolio in the Age of AI

AI changes the economics of execution.

Execution gets cheaper. Attention does not.

That means portfolio management becomes more important, not less.

## Old world
Scarcity lived in implementation capacity.

## New world
Scarcity lives in:
- strategic clarity
- review attention
- integration bandwidth
- decision quality
- willingness to kill bad work fast

## Implication
Teams should raise the bar for what enters the portfolio, even while increasing the speed of what gets executed inside it.

## Portfolio rules for AI-native orgs
- more ideas can be explored cheaply
- fewer bets should be scaled without evidence
- review capacity is the real bottleneck
- telemetry should decide continuation, not enthusiasm
- kill low-signal work earlier

## Failure pattern
AI-native teams often become idea factories. They mistake easier execution for more justification to start things.

That is how attention gets fragmented and quality collapses.
